What is InnoSphere - Bridging AI Horizons for Future Work?

InnoSphere is a pioneering initiative organized by Research Society for IT & AI (ReSITAI) and Greencom Africa, held in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. This event delves deep into the ever-evolving landscape of AI and its implications for the future of work. By bringing together industry experts, thought leaders, and innovators, InnoSphere aims to foster insightful discussions, showcase AI applications in practical scenarios, and catalyze collaborative endeavors. Join us as we explore the intersection of AI, technology, and the dynamic nature of work, paving the way for a progressive and sustainable future.
